Game Developer Club

Game developer club is hosted every Monday at Spokane Public Library.

Schedule:

  • Monday, August 5th, 3pm to 5pm @ South Hill
  • Game Developer Club will be back 2025, email me at cstuart@spokanelibrary.org and I can email you the new dates when they become available.

Welcome

The goal of this group is to work together to learn how to become better game developers, network, collaborate, and learn. We focus on an overall group project for ideas but welcome people to show up with their own projects to work on as well.

If you have any questions reach out to Colin who runs the club at cstuart@spokanelibrary.org
